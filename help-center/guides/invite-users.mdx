---
title: Inviting Team Members
description: Add team members to your C3 Access workspace.
---

## Overview

Invite colleagues to collaborate on your organization.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team Settings">
    From the left menu, select **Team > Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Invite User'">
    Enter the colleague's name and email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set permissions">
    Choose their role (Admin, Viewer, Editor).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invite">
    The user receives an email to join your C3 Access workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>
