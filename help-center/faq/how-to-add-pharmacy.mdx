title: How do I add a new pharmacy?
description: Guide to adding new pharmacies in C3 Access.
---

## Overview

You can add new pharmacy organizations to your C3 Access dashboard easily.

## Steps

1. Log in to your C3 Access dashboard
2. Navigate to **Pharmacies**
3. Click **Add Pharmacy**
4. Fill out pharmacy details (name, address, license info)
5. Click **Save**

You will see the new pharmacy listed. Changes are effective immediately.
